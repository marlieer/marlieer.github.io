Homecare Hub Care Platform

This web app helps seniors find home care in their area and manages billing. It also provides access to adult day programs and alternative shared living and care houses.

Users can search for homecare agencies in their area using the search engine. They can use the map to browse adult day programs or shared living and care in their area. Agencies use the system to manage their patients and billing. Partners can use the site to manage their patients and find care for them on behalf of the patient.

MariaDB | Laravel | VueJS | Google maps API | AWS EC2 | AWS Cloudfront | SEO | Vuex

Nature Fun Summer Scheduler

Scout Island Nature Centre is a non-profit environmental educational centre located in Williams Lake, BC Canada. Each summer, Scout Island employs summer students to develop and run summer programs for school-aged children, full of outdoor fun and learning.

In the past the summer staff register children with pencil and paper, resulting in hours of repetitive flipping through the registration binder to sign children up.

This website eliminates the repitition in registering repeat sessions for children by providing both parents and staff a web interface to register children for available summer sessions. Parents can view sessions online and register their children in available spots. They also have a dashboard summarizing upcoming sessions. Staff can register children and print reports of daily attendance from the site.

PostgreSQL | Laravel | Full Calendar Plugin

Health and Safety Dashboard

This web app helps admin at School District 27 create digital Health & Safety forms that can be assigned to staff or sites with recurring deadlines to complete.

There is flexibility to change form deadlines to be once or on a flexible recurring schedule. Administrators can assign each form to Microsoft Office365 user groups, individual users, or specific sites (for example, all elementary schools). Administrators can quickly run reports on schools and individuals who are overdue for deadlines and export results into Excel spreadsheets.

Users can login to the system using their school district Microsoft credentials, view a calendar with their upcoming or overdue deadlines and complete forms assigned to them. Principals can view all their upcoming deadlines, including deadlines for the school or site they are at.

PostgreSQL | Laravel | Microsoft OAuth integration | Full Calendar Plugin

Screenshots and live demo are unavailable for privacy reasons.

Medical Information Reporting

Doctors want assurance that they are not missing important details during medical examinations and procedures. Current methods of reporting inlude dictations, typing or writing notes by hand.

This web app allows doctors to quickly and easily record observations via interactive features such as radio buttons, checkboxes and sliders. It acts like a digital checklist for their exams. Two written reports will automatically be generated, saving time with reporting: one report with medical jargon for the benefit of the doctor and other health care practitioners, the other for the patient.

ExpressJS | ReactJS | PostgreSQL | JWT

Educational Video Recommender

This research project is meant to develop a personalized recommender system for YouTube videos as a supplementary tool for university students to use to help them learn important classroom concepts.

The system initially collects and ranks YouTube videos on educational classroom topics and displays them to students. Once students using the system interact with the displayed videos by liking and disliking the videos, the system combines the user history with collaborative filtering to produce a personalized list of recommended YouTube videos.

Since the focus of the project was the recommending algorithm, little time was spent on the UI or website performance. A copy of the final research report has been linked below.
